What is MoneroMesh?

MoneroMesh is an open-source dashboard that provides real-time insights into our own Monero node and P2Pool infrastructure — built to promote transparency and decentralization.

Why it matters

By making our infrastructure visible and easy to monitor, we aim to build trust and encourage participation in the Monero ecosystem without relying on centralized platforms.

What we offer:

A public and open-source web dashboard displaying mining activity, block data, and node status — directly from our own deployment.
