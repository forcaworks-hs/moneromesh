What is MoneroMesh?

MoneroMesh is an open-source web dashboard that enables independent monitoring of your Monero infrastructure without relying on centralized services.

Why it matters

Our goal is to offer easy-to-use tools so anyone running their own node and P2Pool can monitor their operations effortlessly, promoting decentralization and transparency.

What you get:

An open-source observer tool to track mining activity in real time, plus the freedom to host your own instance fully connected to your node and pool.
